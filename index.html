Roger Dingledine

"If you care about privacy online, you actively need to protect it" -Roger Dingledine

Roger Dingledine is a American computer scientist born in 1978 well known for his contributions to the Tor Project, an open source, anonymous, and censorship-free web browser that Roger co-founded in 2006, and Mixminion, a secure and anonymous email system.
Tor Browser, known better as "The Deep Web", remains a beacon of freedom for the Internet, helping especially in countries like Iraq or China where an oppressive government suppresses freedom of speech and expression.
